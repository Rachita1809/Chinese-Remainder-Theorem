CHINESE REMAINDER THEOREM


AIM: To understand and implement the Chinese Remainder Thorem with the help of simulation.


THEORY: 1] CRT is used for Secret sharing which consists of distributing a set of shares among a group of people.

2] The set of shares is known as congruant equation.

3] Thus CRT theorem states that there always exists "n" that satisfies a set of congruant equation.

4] Set of congruant equations :X= a1(mod m1), X= a2(mod m2), Xk= ak(mod mk).

5] Thus get the value of X,M1,M2...Mk should be co prime or relative co prime numbers.

6] CRT can also be used during signing of Https certificates.


Theorem developed by Chinese mathematician Sun-tzu
